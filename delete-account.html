Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 5, 2026

You can permanently delete your CookE account and all associated data directly from within the app.

What gets deleted

  • Your account — Your sign-in credentials are removed from our authentication system. You will no longer be able to sign in with the same Google account.
  • Usage history — All daily usage records stored on our servers are deleted.
  • Profile & preferences — Your user profile and any server-side preferences are permanently removed.
  • Local device data — Auth tokens, saved recipes, and dietary preferences stored on your device are cleared. Any remaining local data can be removed by uninstalling the app.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the CookE app and sign in with your Google account.
  2. Tap your avatar icon in the top-right corner to open your profile.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

The deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Need help?

If you no longer have access to the app and need your account deleted manually, please contact us at support@cookeapp.com with the email address associated with your account and we will process the request within 30 days.
